City in a Nutshell

Frankfort is the capital city of the U.S. state of Kentucky and the seat of Franklin County. It is a home rule-class city. The city is located in the inner Bluegrass region of Kentucky and is classified as an urban area, as it is the principal city of the Franfort,Kentucky Micropolitan Statistical Area, which includes all of Franklin and Anderson counties.
